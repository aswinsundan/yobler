Types of Stock Analyst Ratings

There are several types of stock analyst ratings, including buy, sell, hold, and neutral. A buy rating indicates that the analyst believes the stock will outperform the market, while a sell rating suggests that the stock will underperform. A hold rating is neutral, implying that the stock will perform in line with the market, while a neutral rating is similar to a hold rating, but may indicate a lack of conviction or uncertainty about the stock’s future performance.
